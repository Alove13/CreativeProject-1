Brief Intro to HoloLens

HoloLens is a Augmented Reality Head Mount Display (HMD) produced by Microsoft. It enables the user to still be present in the real world, while bringing the virtual world into theirs!

Although, HoloLens is available to developers, it sells at a mighty $3,000! Basically, if you are well funded or work at Microsoft, then go for it! Otherwise, maybe try a HMD that is a bit cheaper, or stick with Virtual Reality for starters! Either way, it is a great world to enter!
